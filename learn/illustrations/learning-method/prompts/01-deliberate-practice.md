# Illustration: Deliberate Practice Comparison

## Visual Concept
A side-by-side comparison showing two different approaches to practice.

## Left Side: "一般人" (Regular Person)
- A student happily doing easy problems they already know
- Comfort zone bubble around them (cozy, warm colors)
- Small upward arrow showing slow progress
- Repeated checkmarks on easy problems
- Relaxed, comfortable expression

## Right Side: "高手" (Expert)
- A student tackling difficult problems they don't know yet
- Stepping outside comfort zone (dynamic pose)
- Large upward arrow showing rapid progress
- X marks turning into checkmarks (learning process)
- Determined, focused expression

## Composition
- Clear vertical divider in center
- Left side: warm orange/yellow tones (comfortable but stagnant)
- Right side: energetic teal/green tones (challenging but growing)
- Simple icons: checkmarks, arrows, lightbulbs
- Child-friendly cartoon style, no realistic faces

## Style Details
- Warm, friendly illustration style
- Soft rounded shapes
- Pastel background with gentle gradients
- Simple character silhouettes (iconic, not detailed)
- Encouraging, positive mood
- Suitable for 6th grade students

## Text Elements (optional)
- Top left: "舒適圈內" (Inside Comfort Zone)
- Top right: "跳出舒適圈" (Outside Comfort Zone)
